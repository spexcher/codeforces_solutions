Visitors stay for $$$d$$$ continuous days, all of those $$$d$$$ days must be between day $$$1$$$ and $$$n$$$ inclusive.</p><p>Robin has a total of $$$k$$$ risky 'jobs' planned. The $$$i$$$-th job takes place between days $$$l_i$$$ and $$$r_i$$$ inclusive, for $$$1 \le i \le k$$$.
